For years I’ve been looking for a good way to keep in touch with people. Recently, it’s started to look like email is one of the best ways to do this after all, so I’m going to try out an email list.

My goal would be to send out emails, usually never more often that once per week, and probably something like once per month. If nothing else, I’d like a way to tell everyone Merry Christmas! (Seriously).

Here’s some of what I’d anticipate sending.

  • Links to latest blog posts
  • Any updates on personal news (like I have a new job).
  • Any important updates on family news
  • Maybe a question looking for some input

There’s nothing going on here other than me looking for the best way to keep in touch with friends and family.
