Crash Course in Astronomy

Tue 20 Dec 2016 14:49 EST

I’m not always a fan of Phil Plait, but he usually does an outstanding job of explaining astronomy. This course on Youtube of bite-sized topics are quite well done.

I’m also not a fan of cramming all of Phil’s statments together in a packed video. It’s frenetic and wears on me after a bit but, taken in small doses, the content is good and the editing is bearable.
